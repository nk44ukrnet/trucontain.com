TruContain® Garage Floor Containment Mats

TruContain® is the leading, no assembly required containment mat in North America. TruContain® garage floor containment mats are designed to keep snow, mud, ice, oil, water, and most other dirt and fluids from destroying your garage floor – and the valuable items you store in your garage.

TruContain® is available in both the original grade and the TruContain® HD Heavy Duty containment mat – which weights 33% more than the original product. New for 2020 TruContain® auto floor mats contain free 3M® Velcro corners and continue to include the squeegee head.

We offer free shipping to the contiguous USA and a flat rate shipping to Canada. The best part of our Canadian shipping program is that all duties, taxes, brokerage, GST and PST are all prepaid. That means the delivery company will never ask you for additional funds. What you see is what you pay! We now offer expedited shipping to many parts of the country. Transit times are not guaranteed and we do not offer refunds on shipping costs.

TruContain® HD

While competitors are making their containment mats thinner and using the term HD to describe them, our original TruContain mat remains at its' original weight. By popular demand we launched the TruContain HD mat for people who live in Canada, Alaska and parts of the continental US where having gravel stuck in the tires is common. While it is more expensive it is also 1/3rd heavier and outlasts our standard product and the competition.

Why Do I need a Containment Mat

Many Americans drive into their garage with their car covered in snow – or have to stand outside in the freezing cold getting all the packed on snow off. Once they pull into the garage the snow melts. The melting snow creates a dangerous, dirty, wet mess across the garage floor. Often that dirt gets tracked into your home or destroys items being stored in the garage. TruContain is a huge value year-round. It traps mud in spring and summer, dirt, oil and other automotive fluids.
